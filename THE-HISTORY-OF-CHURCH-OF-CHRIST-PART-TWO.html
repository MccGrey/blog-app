THE HISTORY OF CHURCH OF CHRIST PART TWO
TOPIC: APOSTASY – A THEMATIC ANALYSIS

by: Innocent Agu
Jan 23, 2024 || 12:45
QUARTERLY THEME: THE HISTORY OF CHURCH OF CHRIST PART TWO:
TOPIC: APOSTASY – A THEMATIC ANALYSIS

2 Thessalonians 2:1-3,7  
3 Now we beseech you, brethren, by the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ, and by our gathering together unto him, 2  That ye be not soon shaken in mind, or be troubled, neither by spirit, nor by word, nor by letter as from us, as that the day of Christ is at hand. 3  Let no man deceive you by any means: for that day shall not come, except there come a falling away first, and that man of sin be revealed, the son of perdition. … 7  For the mystery of iniquity doth already work: only he who now letteth will let, until he be taken out of the way.


INTRODUCTION:

We have been taking a series of studies on the Establishment, Apostasy and Restoration of the Christian Church. We focused on the establishment of the Church in the previous quarter which x-rayed various prophecies concerning the establishment of the Christian Church and the eventual establishment of the church. In this quarter, we shall be looking at the History of Church Apostasy (Falling away from the New Testament pattern of Christianity) and our next quarter theme will focus on Reformation and Restoration Movements. Our references will be to the Bible primarily and then, to the book, ‘The Eternal Kingdom: A History Of The Church Of Christ’ by F.W. Mattox, PhD who was The President of Lubbock Christian College Lubbock, Texas. His book is largely based on the History of the Christian Church by Eusebius who recorded his historical account of the Christian Church around the fourth Century AD and who was an eyewitness of most of the accounts he recorded.

The study will also look into some symbolic representations of some of the events of Church apostasy in the Book of Revelations such as the Dragon, the seven-headed beast and the harlot that rides on the back of the seven-headed beast, the lamb-like beast that speaks like a dragon which made an image for the previous seven-headed beast and caused the people to worship that previous beast whose wounds now seemed to be healed. We shall try to link these symbols to the actual historical events.

Having this general overview about the Christian Church will help solidify our understanding of Christian religion and this study is very important for every professing Christian whether you are a member of Church of Christ or not. The study will x-ray events that happened in and around the Christian church from as early as the first century AD. The chronicles of post-apostolic events will be studied and these include the internal and external forces that troubled the Church in these early periods, the blackmail of the Christian Church by Nero which sparked up the early resentments towards the church and the subsequent severe state persecutions that followed. It will also highlight some early deviations from New Testament pattern and look into the influence of Constantine which led to the merger of the church with the pagan state of Rome called the Roman Empire which turned the church into an empirical church that became the official reference point of Church Apostasy. The key doctrinal deviations and the early resistance by various individuals and groups and the second round of persecutions in form of Crusades by then Romanized empirical church will also be discussed.

The next quarter will x-ray efforts of some prominent individuals or groups that moved to reform the apostate Roman empirical church and how some of them ended up being killed or exiled while some of them also ended up forming new religious groups with yet the same doctrinal deviations rather than seeking to restore the original church from which Roman Empirical Church was formed. It will also look into the restoration movements that eventually restored the New Testament church in accordance to the call in Revelations 18:4 which says, “And I heard another voice from heaven, saying, Come out of her, my people, that ye be not partakers of her sins, and that ye receive not of her plagues.” The restoration will be in areas of names or designations, organizational system, mode of membership, worship pattern and other key religious beliefs and practices.

Remember that the Holy Spirit spoke in 2 Thessalonians 2:3 thus “for that day shall not come, except there come a falling away first”. It is evident that this prophecy has been fulfilled a long time ago and the onus on every professing Christian to study and know on which side of Christianity he is in: The Apostate Church or The Restored Church.

In this thematic analysis, we should ask some basic questions: What is Apostasy, why did it happen and why did God allow it to happen in his church?


WHAT IS APOSTASY?

Advanced English Dictionary defines APOSTASY in two related ways as follow:
1. The state of having rejected your religious beliefs or your political party or a cause (often in favour of opposing beliefs or causes).

2. The act of abandoning a party for a cause.

These are the ways the English Dictionary defines the term APOSTASY. In addition to the above definitions, Bible defines apostasy as “FALLING AWAY OR DEPARTURE FROM THE FAITH OR BEING DRAWN AWAY INTO HERESY”.

2 Thessalonians 2:3  
Let no man deceive you by any means: for that day shall not come, except there come a falling away first…
1 Timothy 4:1-2  Now the Spirit speaketh expressly, that in the latter times some shall depart from the faith…, giving heed to seducing spirits, and doctrines of devils.
2 Peter 2:1-3  
1 But there were false prophets also among the people, even as there shall be false teachers among you, who privily shall bring in damnable heresies, even denying the Lord that bought them, and bring upon themselves swift destruction. 2  And many shall follow their pernicious ways…

All the above prophecies show that apostasy from the true Christian Faith was not an accident at all. God knew about it before it happened. God warned us adequately about it in the so those that would ignore his warnings and fall victims will meet God in judgment.


WHY DID APOSTASY HAPPEN?

There are many possible factors that made apostasy possible but let us mention but a few:  Love for self-pleasure: Quest for leisure does not allow people to choose between religious entertainments or fantasies and true worship. This makes it easy for them to accept any teaching so far it comes with material comfort.

2 Timothy 3:1-2
 This know also, that in the last days perilous times shall come. 2  For men shall be lovers of their own selves, covetous, boasters, proud, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy, (also see Ephesians 5:19, Hebrews 13:15) Ephesians 4:14
 That we henceforth be no more children, tossed to and fro, and carried about with every wind of doctrine, by the sleight of men, and cunning craftiness, whereby they lie in wait to deceive;

 Quest for materialism:

People’s urge to get rich quickly and miraculously has made them not to pay attentions to bible doctrines but to motivational speeches that promise them wealth and earthly success. Quest to make money also pushes many religious leaders into floating privately owned churches that will give them absolute control and access to church money. When a greedy man meets a greedy man, the result is unspeakably ugly Philippians 3:18-19
 (For many walk, of whom I have told you often, and now tell you even weeping, that they are the enemies of the cross of Christ: 19  Whose end is destruction, whose God is their belly, and whose glory is in their shame, who mind earthly things.) . (also see Romans 16:16-18) 

Hardness of Hearts:

2 Timothy 4:3-4  For the time will come when they will not endure sound doctrine; but after their own lusts shall they heap to themselves teachers, having itching ears; 4  And they shall turn away their ears from the truth, and shall be turned unto fables. (also see 2 Thessalonians 2:9-12)

 Satan’s quest to push men to hellfire.

Revelation 12:12
 Therefore rejoice, ye heavens, and ye that dwell in them. Woe to the inhabiters of the earth and of the sea! for the devil is come down unto you, having great wrath, because he knoweth that he hath but a short time. (also see 1 Peter 5:8)


WHY DID GOD ALLOW APOSTASY TO HAPPEN TO HIS CHURCH?

God created man with the freedom to will. That was why He put the Tree of Life in the Garden of Eden and also the Tree of Knowledge of Good and Evil and commanded Adam not to eat from the later tree. God wanted them to have the freedom to make their own choices so they would be responsible for any path they chose for themselves.

It is the same thing with our generation. No one is forced into apostasy. Everyone has the power of will to accept or reject deviations from the Word of God. In early days of Roman Catholic, Pope ordered the killings of some Christians that rejected his authority and those disciples were willing to die than to submit to the Pope.

Apostasy was not brought by God but by Satan. People are encouraged to study the word of God in order not to fall prey of Satan’s antics but not many of them are willing to listen. This apostasy will show those who are truly loyal to God and those that are rebellious, who would willfully abandon the commandments of God because of their own material lusts and pleasures. God always allows people to make their own choices so they will be fully responsible for their actions.

Philippians 2:
…work out your own salvation with fear and trembling.
Matthew 16:27
 For the Son of man shall come in the glory of his Father with his angels; and then he shall reward every man according to his works.


HIGHLIGHTS OF THE THINGS WE SHALL COVER IN THIS THEME

THE STATE OF THE CHURCH BEFORE APOSTASY:-
This will help us know the internal and external forces trying to destabilize the church prior to eventual apostasy. (2 Corinthians 7:5, 1 John 2:18-19)

PROPHECIES CONCERNING THE APOSTASY:-
This will help us know various prophecies and warnings that were given to early Christians to prepare their minds for the eventual persecutions that would come upon the church which would ultimately lead to other issues that paved the way for departures from the New Testament Christian religious system into a compromised Christian religious system. (Revelations 1:1)

UNDERSTANDING THE PROPHECY ABOUT THE COMING OF THE MAN OF SIN IN 2 THESSALONIANS 2:1-4 AND THE STRATEGIC IMPACT HIS EMERGENCE WOULD HAVE ON THE GLOBAL CHRISTIAN COMMUNITY:-

This will help us know about the emergence of a singular highest Christian religious office that would have all churches submit to his authority as an overall person in the church whose rule would lead the church into damnable perdition.

UNDERSTANDING THE VISION OF THE BEASTS AND THE PROSTITUTE:-



This will help us to understand the symbolic representations of Satan who transferred his throne and power to the empire that ruled the world at that time who in turn transferred that same throne and power to the religious empire that replaced it and would make his collapsed empire to become relevant again through religion. (Rev. 12, 13, 17, 18)

FROM PERSECUTION TO AN ILLUSIONARY PEACE (NOT VICTORY, BUT A CHANGE OF TACTICS):-

 This will examined how the church was severely persecuted to stamp it out and how the perseverance of the church led to the Edict of Milan. (Revelations 13:7)

THE UNHOLY UNION: (THE INFLUENCE OF CONSTANTINE AND THE CHURCH’S MERGER WITH THE ROMAN STATE):-

 This will tell us how Constantine’s merger of the Church with idol-worshipping Roman Empire gave the church the final blow of doctrinal and organizational compromise which is the actual apostasy the prophecies about falling away from the faith point to. (Revelations 18:1-5)

SOME KEY DEPARTURES FROM THE ORIGINAL FAITH BY NOW APOSTATE CHURCH:-

This will highlight some of the major or key departures from the New Testament doctrines and practices in the areas of Salvation (infant and sprinkling baptism), areas of church organization (formation of Parishes and overall one-man rule), areas of eternity (creation of a false second chance after death in form of Purgatory, Penance, Sale of indulgence, etc.), in the area of church officers (enacting of the law of celibacy, special robes for the clergy, creation of unscriptural titles, etc.), (1 timothy 4:1-3) 

THE CRUSADES: CRUSHING OF OPPOSITIONS TO THE AUTHORITY OF THE ROMAN PONTIFF:-

This will discuss some of key crusades and executions of people and groups that opposed the authority of the Roman Pontiff and other doctrinal practices of the empirical Church. Some were burnt alive, some were massacred, some were exiled and had their properties confiscated just the same way the earlier Roman Emperors persecuted the early church before Edict of Milan. (Revelation 13:11-12, 17)

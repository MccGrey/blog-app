About Me

BIBLE STUDY WITH INNOCENT is an independent Bible-based Media Evangelism and Research aimed at:

Reaching the world with the pure and unperverted gospel of Christ.
Correcting some dangerous doctrines and practices of many churches that are contrary to the teachings of Christ and the inspired Bible authors.
Tracing the Bible -based African historical heritage with regards to the true Bible lands, Rivers and landmarks.
Discovering the dispersions of the twelve tribes of Israel in Africa according to Zephaniah 3:10.
Carry out further Bible-based research on some other areas not mentioned above.. . . The programme is run and sponsored by volunteers from Churches of Christ all over the world through an independent Media Evangelism Ministry named Family Bible Ministry.
The Anchor of the programme is Innocent Chukwudi Agu who has been a Media Evangelist of a long standing, having been the first Church of Christ Television Evangelism presenter in Nigeria's Federal Capital Territory, Abuja from 2010. Until today, he remains one of the presenters of the popular Let The Bible Speak Television programme on Independent Television (ITV) Abuja which is a programme sponsored by Churches of Christ in Abuja and its environs that airs every Saturday from 6:00pm to 6:30pm.
Family Bible Ministry which owns this project also prints and distributes Bible-based Literatures, Movies, Hymns, etc. and welcomes donations from well-meaning Christians all over the world. Whatever you put into the preaching of the gospel of Christ is an investment in heaven whose reward must surely come. We will be glad to have you as our partner in the kingdom work.
The Churches of Christ salute you. (Romans 16:16).

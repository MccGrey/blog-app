THE HISTORY OF CHURCH OF CHRIST PART ONE: ESTABLISHMENT, APOSTASY & RESTORATION – A THEMATIC ANALYSIS

by: Innocent Agu
Jan 9th, 2024 || 09:40

INTRODUCTION:

Every disciple of Christ, Christian, believer in Christ, born again, etc. must belong to the true church. The Christian Church started in Jerusalem about 33AD as one universal church and they all practiced one form of doctrine globally and were identified with the same names globally.

Unfortunately, during the period of their oneness, the Holy Spirit spoke in prophecy that false teachers and prophets would arise from within and outside that church and form more Christian groups which they would deceive and exploit.

The Holy Spirit also spoke about the sudden spiritual decay of the symbolic Babylon and admonished the faithful believers to depart from her to avoid sharing in her eternal punishment.

This provides the world with tremendous challenge of identifying the false churches talked about, together with the false prophets, the symbolic Babylon and strive not to be in their camps to avoid eternal destruction. The only way we can identify the false churches and prophets is by knowing the facts about the true Church with regards to its history, establishment, identity, pattern of worship, pattern of organisation and other vital facts. We should not advocate for the wrong churches to be left alone to exist because Ephesians 5:11 says:

And have no fellowship with the unfruitful works of darkness, but rather reprove them.

It is futile to hope that God would overlook their wrongs because he never did so in the past . He did not overlook the wrongs of the generation of Noah before the flood. He did not overlook the wrongs of Sodom and Gomorrah and their neighbouring cities. He did not overlook the wrongs of Israel his loved nation. He will not also overlook the wrongs of false prophets and false churches.

This theme is therefore designed to take us down the memory lane of the historical facts about the one true bible church from its founding in 33AD to the present day with regards to its establishment, apostasy and restoration. May God bless us as we study.

ESTABLISHMENT OF THE CHURCH OF CHRIST

This has to do with ancient plans of God for the establishment of Church of Christ which include:

- The prophecies concerning its establishment in Jerusalem.

Daniel 2:44 And in the days of these kings shall the God of heaven set up a kingdom, which shall never be destroyed: and the kingdom shall not be left to other people, but it shall break in pieces and consume all these kingdoms, and it shall stand for ever.

Micah 4:1-2 1 But in the last days it shall come to pass, that the mountain of the house of the LORD shall be established in the top of the mountains, and it shall be exalted above the hills; and people shall flow unto it.  2 And many nations shall come, and say, Come, and let us go up to the mountain of the LORD, and to the house of the God of Jacob; and he will teach us of his ways, and we will walk in his paths: for the law shall go forth of Zion, and the word of the LORD from Jerusalem.

- The prayer concerning the coming of the kingdom of Christ or of God and the revelation of God’s will in form of the New Testament Bible.

Mathew 6:10 Thy kingdom come. Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.

- The promise of Christ to build his church and imminent take-off of his kingdom or church.

Mathew 16:18 And I say also unto thee, That thou art Peter, and upon this rock I will build my church; and the gates of hell shall not prevail against it.

Mark 9:1 And he said unto them, Verily I say unto you, That there be some of them that stand here, which shall not taste of death, till they have seen the kingdom of God come with power.

- The actual establishment of the church or kingdom in Jerusalem about 33AD. Acts 2:1-42, Colossians 1:13

- The worship and organization of the church established in Jerusalem by Christ.

- Worship Acts 2:42 & 47

- Organization Acts 14:23, Titus 1:5, Philippians 1:1

THE APOSTASY OF THE CHURCH


This includes:

- Prophecies concerning departures from the established church.

1 Timothy 4:1 Now the Spirit speaketh expressly, that in the latter times some shall depart from the faith, giving heed to seducing spirits, and doctrines of devils

Acts 20:29-30 29 For I know this, that after my departing shall grievous wolves enter in among you, not sparing the flock.  30 Also of your own selves shall men arise, speaking perverse things, to draw away disciples after them.

2 Timothy 4:3-4 3 For the time will come when they will not endure sound doctrine; but after their own lusts shall they heap to themselves teachers, having itching ears;  4 and they shall turn away their ears from the truth, and shall be turned unto fables.

Galatians 1:6-7 6 I marvel that ye are so soon removed from him that called you into the grace of Christ unto another gospel:  7 which is not another; but there be some that trouble you, and would pervert the gospel of Christ.

2 Peter 2:1-3 1 But there were false prophets also among the people, even as there shall be false teachers among you, who privily shall bring in damnable heresies, even denying the Lord that bought them, and bring upon themselves swift destruction.  2 And many shall follow their pernicious ways; by reason of whom the way of truth shall be evil spoken of.  3 And through covetousness shall they with feigned words make merchandise of you: whose judgment now of a long time lingereth not, and their damnation slumbereth not.

- History of actual apostasy and areas of apostasy such as:

- Name: Ignatius of Antioch first used the word Catholic and after Roman Empire took over the church and mixed it with their government, they invented a lot of doctrines and practices and named them CATHOLIC hence the name Roman Catholic Church was officially born and prophecy of apostasy officially fulfilled.

- Organization: Every church had ordained local elders but the now Roman Catholic Church cum Empire exalted Roman bishop above all bishops and had all the Roman churches subjected under his rule thus fulfilling the prophecy of the Man of Sin.

2 Thessalonians 2:3-4 3 Let no man deceive you by any means: for that day shall not come, except there come a falling away first, and that man of sin be revealed, the son of perdition;  4 who opposeth and exalteth himself above all that is called God, or that is worshipped; so that he as God sitteth in the temple of God, shewing himself that he is God

THE RESTORATION OF THE CHURCH


This includes:

- Bible calls for the separation of faithful Christians from the apostate Church symbolized as the fallen Babylon the Great.

Revelations 18:1-5 1 And after these things I saw another angel come down from heaven, having great power; and the earth was lightened with his glory.  2 And he cried mightily with a strong voice, saying, Babylon the great is fallen, is fallen, and is become the habitation of devils, and the hold of every foul spirit, and a cage of every unclean and hateful bird.  3 For all nations have drunk of the wine of the wrath of her fornication, and the kings of the earth have committed fornication with her, and the merchants of the earth are waxed rich through the abundance of her delicacies.

4 And I heard another voice from heaven, saying, Come out of her, my people, that ye be not partakers of her sins, and that ye receive not of her plagues.  5 For her sins have reached unto heaven, and God hath remembered her iniquities.

- The reformation movements that gave rise to more denominations and further apostasies which is the reason the symbolic Babylon is called the mother of all harlots.

Revelations 17:4-6 4 And the woman was arrayed in purple and scarlet colour, and decked with gold and precious stones and pearls, having a golden cup in her hand full of abominations and filthiness of her fornication:  5 and upon her forehead was a name written, MYSTERY, BABYLON THE GREAT, THE MOTHER OF HARLOTS AND ABOMINATIONS OF THE EARTH.  6 And I saw the woman drunken with the blood of the saints, and with the blood of the martyrs of Jesus: and when I saw her, I wondered with great admiration.

Lesson on reformers will feature some reformers like:

Martin Luther of Lutheran Church, John Calvin of Presbyterian Church, John Smith of Baptist Church, John Wesley of Methodist Church and some others.

The restoration movements that eventually restored the New Testament Church in names, worship, organization, etc.

This will include movements championed by the likes of:

Thomas Campbell and his son Alexander Campbell, J.W. Stone, Abner Jones, etc.

CONCLUSION:

Not knowing the history of the true church will make it difficult to identify and reject false prophets and false churches because all denominational churches and all human invented doctrines are not approved by God.

1 John 4:1,6 1 Beloved, believe not every spirit, but try the spirits whether they are of God: because many false prophets are gone out into the world. ... 6 We are of God: he that knoweth God heareth us; he that is not of God heareth not us. Hereby know we the spirit of truth, and the spirit of error.

Mathew 15:8-9 8 This people draweth nigh unto me with their mouth, and honoureth me with their lips; But their heart is far from me. 9 But in vain they do worship me, Teaching for doctrines the commandments of men.

Restoration therefore entails that we should employ people to reject religious divisions or denominations and embrace unity of all true believers both in fellowship and doctrines.

Luke 11:23 He that is not with me is against me: and he that gathereth not with me scattereth.

This is because, on the last day judgement, a lot of churches or professing Christians will be rejected by Christ according to what he already said as shown below.

Mathew 7:21-23 21 Not every one that saith unto me, Lord, Lord, shall enter into the kingdom of heaven; but he that doeth the will of my Father which is in heaven.  22 Many will say to me in that day, Lord, Lord, have we not prophesied in thy name? and in thy name have cast out devils? and in thy name done many wonderful works?  23 And then will I profess unto them, I never knew you: depart from me, ye that work iniquity.

UNDERSTANDING THE MYSTERY OF THE BEASTS AND THE HARLOT

by: Innocent Agu
Jan 31, 2024 || 10:45

INTRODUCTION

In the Book of Revelation 1:1, we read:  

“The Revelation of Jesus Christ, which God gave unto him, to shew unto his servants things which must shortly come to pass; and he sent and signified it by his angel unto his servant John:”

God sent a message to the Christian world through the seven churches in Asia Minor telling them about the events that would shortly happen in the world which would affect the Christian religion and he signified those events with symbolic images. It therefore requires critical bible studies to understand the messages the symbols convey.

Because the book of Revelations is highly symbolic, some Christian teachers have portrayed it as a “NO GO AREA” but I don’t belong to that school of thought. God in the book of 2 Timothy 2:15  demands that we should “Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.”

This is because, the mystery and the manifold wisdom of God is made known by the church. (Ephesians 3:9-10). If therefore, the church should shy away from the book of Revelations, then, which group of people should study it and make use of the knowledge it contains? Deuteronomy 29:29 says that the hidden things belong to God but the things that are revealed, belong to us. Revelations is therefore a book of revealed things and therefore belongs to us. Since the book of Revelations is a message to the church, the church must seek to understand it and not to run away from it.


THE OBJECTIVE OF THIS LESSON

God delivered the Book of Revelations as a message to the seven churches of Asia and in the world. The aim of the Book of Revelations would be defeated if the church understands nothing in the book.

John the Apostle saw visions of things that would affect the history of Christianity in Revelations 12-13 and 17-18. This lesson seeks to explain those things and how they impacted the Christian religious history. The knowledge will help in building our faith in Christ and belief of the truth; as our salvation is not only dependent on knowing that Jesus Christ is the Lord and Saviour and accepting him, but also dependent on our belief of the truth. If we don’t know what the truth is, how can we know what to believe and what to reject?

“But we are bound to give thanks alway to God for you, brethren beloved of the Lord, because God hath from the beginning chosen you to salvation through sanctification of the Spirit and belief of the truth.” (2 Thessalonians 2:13)

If one does not understand the book of Revelations, he is likely to be deceived and damned for all eternity hence, the need for this lesson


THE THINGS TO STUDY IN THIS LESSON

There are four symbolic beasts we shall study in this lesson with a view to unraveling what they signify. They include:

1. The red Dragon with seven heads and ten horns.

2. The Leopard with seven heads and ten horns.

3. The beast with the appearance of a lamb.

4. The scarlet (red) beast with seven heads and ten horns and the harlot that sits on it.

Our objective is to understand what these figures represent and how they affect the Christian religion. All the symbolic images are great bible lessons packaged by our God for the church. Our God is a great teacher; He used those symbols as teaching aids to simplify and summarize the lessons about historical events that would affect the Christian religion. If the church fails to understand those symbols, the messages would be lost and the purpose of writing the book would be defeated.






THE KEY TO UNDERSTANDING THE MYSTERY OF THE BEASTS

The visions of Revelations 12 – 13 and Revelations 17-18 point to the events of history of the church. One beast that is central to the events and to which other beasts and the harlot are linked to, is the beast with seven heads and its trade mark TEN HORNS.

This beast in Revelation 17:3 and Revelations 13:1 had earlier been represented in Daniel’s vision of the world Kingdoms or Empires as the fourth beast having ten horns which he said represented the fourth Kingdom. (Dan. 7:23) It is also represented by the fourth segment of the Image of Nebuchadnezzar's dream with TEN TOES which Daniel also described as the fourth Kingdom. (Dan. 2:40)

Seven heads and ten horns are therefore the symbolic representation of the Roman Empire which was the fourth of the four World Empires. The scarlet-coloured beast with seven heads and ten horns and the Leopard with seven heads and ten horns in the book of Revelations refer to the same fourth world Empire – Rome. Other images (the Harlot and the Lamb) in the visions are tied to the influence of the same Roman Empire on the Christian religion.





Revelation 18:2-4 2 And he cried mightily with a strong voice, saying, Babylon the great is fallen, is fallen, and is become the habitation of devils, and the hold of every foul spirit, and a cage of every unclean and hateful bird. 3  For all nations have drunk of the wine of the wrath of her fornication, and the kings of the earth have committed fornication with her, and the merchants of the earth are waxed rich through the abundance of her delicacies. 4  And I heard another voice from heaven, saying, Come out of her, my people, that ye be not partakers of her sins, and that ye receive not of her plagues.

As the woman is the Mother of all Harlots, she introduced religious perversions to Christianity ranging from idol and image worship to enforcement of celibacy, promotion of forgiveness of sins after death in form of Purgatory and Indulgence, corruption of baptism, etc. Later Christian bodies (denominations) would continue with more religious perversions which Rome initiated as the mother of Christian religious perversions. The faithful Christians are warned to come out her perverted religious system and hold fast to the faith once delivered unto the saints. (Jude 3)

1 Timothy 4:1  Now the Spirit speaketh expressly, that in the latter times some shall depart from the faith, giving heed to seducing spirits, and doctrines of devils.

2 Thessalonians 2:15  Therefore, brethren, stand fast, and hold the traditions which ye have been taught, whether by word, or our epistle.












CONCLUSION

THE VISION OF THE WOMAN ON THE SCARLET BEAST It is clear that the woman sitting on the red-coloured beast is the capital city of Roman Empire now represented by the Vatican City while the beast she is sitting on is the Empire itself. The vision is about the religious corruption or apostasy (perdition) that symbolic woman (City) brought upon the Christian religion which makes her the Mother of all harlots. (Rev. 17:5).

THE VISION OF THE DRAGON, LEOPARD AND THE LAMB This summarily tells us the source of Power of the Roman Pontiff represented by the Lamb. The Dragon (Satan) gave his seat and great authority to the Leopard (Roman Empire), After the Leopard, the Lamb (Roman Pontiff) used the same authority of the Leopard (Roman Empire). In all these, Satan’s authority is still what is in display. This is what the Holy Spirit pointed out in the prophecy below:

1 Timothy 4:1  Now the Spirit speaketh expressly, that in the latter times some shall depart from the faith, giving heed to seducing spirits, and doctrines of devils.

Jesus said, “He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the churches” (Revelation 2:29) but if anyone wants to be ignorant, let him be ignorant. I thank you for taking this study journey with me. May God bless you.
